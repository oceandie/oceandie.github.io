I am currenly a senior scientist of the Ocean, Cryosphere and Dangerous Climate Change (OCDC) group of the UK Met Office Hadley Center.

Within the OCDC group, I am part of the Ocean Modelling team, working on the design, development and assessment of the Met Office global and regional NEMO-based ocean models suitable for operational short-term and seasonal to decadal ocean forecasting as well as climate predictions.
