I am a physical oceanographer interested in understanding the mechanisms underpinning the multi-scales dynamics of the oceans. 
My research involves using and contributing to develop numerical ocean models able to realistically simulate the physical processes of the oceans.   

Currently, I am a Scientist at the [UK Met Office](https://www.metoffice.gov.uk). I am part of the [Ocean Forecasting R&D Group](https://www.metoffice.gov.uk/research/approach/our-scientists/ocean-forecasting) and I work on the development and design of the [NEMO](https://www.nemo-ocean.eu/) based [North-West European Shelf](https://www.metoffice.gov.uk/research/weather/ocean-forecasting/high-resolution-regional-ocean-model) ocean model.
