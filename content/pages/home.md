I am a physical oceanographer interested in understanding the mechanisms underpinning the multi-scales dynamics of the oceans.
My research involves using and contributing to develop numerical ocean models able to realistically simulate the physical processes of the oceans. 

Currently, I am a scientist of the [Ocean Forecasting R&D Group](https://www.metoffice.gov.uk/research/approach/our-scientists/ocean-forecasting) of the [UK Met Office](https://www.metoffice.gov.uk). My work focuses on the development, design and assessment of the [NEMO](https://www.nemo-ocean.eu/) based ocean model of the new high resolution ocean-wave coupled forecasting system of the [North-West European Shelf](https://www.metoffice.gov.uk/research/weather/ocean-forecasting/high-resolution-regional-ocean-model) and the development of a new high resolution regional model for the Arabian Sea.
